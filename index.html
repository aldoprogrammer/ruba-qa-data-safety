Deleting Your Account on Pesaantern Ruba'qa App

Following are the steps that you can use to delete your account on the app:

  1. Step 1: Navigate to Your Profile by clicking on the 'Profile' tab from the home screen bottom section to access your user profile settings.
  2. Step 2: Manage Your Profile. Within your profile tab, locate the 'Manage Profile' button. Tapping it will open the Manage Profile section.
  3. Step 3: Delete My Account: In the Manage Profile screen, press the red button titled 'Delete My Account' to initiate the account deletion process.
