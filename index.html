User Data Safety: Our Commitment to Your Privacy

Why Your Data Safety Matters to Us

For a better experience, while using our Service, we may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to First Name, Last Name, Email, Address, City, Country. The information that we request will be retained by us and used as described in this privacy policy.

The app does use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you.

Trust and Transparency

We value the trust you place in us and believe in being transparent about how your data is collected, used, and protected.

Security Measures

Our platform employs robust security measures to prevent unauthorized access, ensuring that your personal information remains confidential.

Compliance with Regulations

We adhere to the guidelines and policies set forth by relevant regulatory bodies, prioritizing compliance with industry standards.

Account Deletion Process

In the case that a user has graduated, no longer attends the Pesaantern Ruba'qa school, or no longer holds a role such as teacher or admin, the deletion of their account may be requested. Users cannot delete their accounts directly; they will need to ask the authorized personnel such as an admin or teacher to process the deletion.

Admins and teachers are authorized to delete accounts of individuals, including those who have resigned from their roles, as long as the person requesting the deletion is still part of the organization.

Our Commitment to Data Policies

  • Data Minimization: We only collect the data necessary for the functionality of our platform, minimizing the collection of personal information.

  • Account Deletion: Authorized personnel, such as admins and teachers, can request and initiate the deletion of user profiles and related data when necessary.
